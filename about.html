About

Bunbury Hire & Sales is a small, locally ran operation providing reliable equipment for local property owners, trades, and rural projects. We focus on practical, well maintained gear that’s easy to pick up, easy to use, and ready when you need it. Our goal is to make hiring or buying equipment simple, transparent, and stress free for everyone in the South West.
